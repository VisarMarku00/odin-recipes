Egg Fried Rice

Description

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup green beans
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. In a saucepan bring water, salt and soy sauce to a boil. Add rice and stir. Remove from heat, cover and let stand 5 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saute onions and green beans for 2 to 3 minutes. Pour in egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.

  3. Step 3

    4. Stir in the cooked rice, mix well and sprinkle with pepper.
